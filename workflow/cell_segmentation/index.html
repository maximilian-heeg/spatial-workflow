Cell segmentation

Our two step approach includes cellpose and baysor.

  • Cellpose creates a segmentation based on the image data.
  • Baysor then used the transcript neighboorhood to refine the segmentation.

Lastly, we put everything together in an Anndata object that can be used for downstream analysis.
